Leadership Development Program

LDP at LCC is intended to help student leaders in their leadership journey, providing assistance, as well as opportunities to grow. Main parts of the program include:

  • Regular trainings
  • weekly 1-1 mentoring meetings
  • weekly team meetings
  • challenge cards
  • leadership workshops
  • team retreats
Although RA position requires a lot of independent work, teamwork is an important part of it as well. We started with our newly formed team in August, going through the preparational training. Ever since, we spend parts of our time in LDP together, attending educational leadership-related workshops, weekly meetings where we discuss the agenda and plans for the future, RA retreats that contribute to the team-building, as well as simply time asking for advice or collaborating on events.
